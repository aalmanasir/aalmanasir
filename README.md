## The Red Thread Stories

**The Red Thread Stories** is a bilingual literary project (English and Arabic) composed of three volumes, presented as one digital reading experience.  
The work explores memory, identity, absence, and human connection.

## The Red Thread Stories

**The Red Thread Stories** is a bilingual literary project in English and Arabic, bringing together three volumes in a single digital reading experience.

Across the trilogy, the writing reflects on absence and belonging, the rhythm of ordinary life, and the ties that remain between people across time and distance.

## Academic Research

My academic research focuses on international commercial arbitration, contract interpretation, and legal legitimacy.

- [**The Ambiguous Contract (DOI)**](https://doi.org/10.13140/RG.2.2.22656.08962) — Master’s research examining the authority of the arbitrator in interpreting the law applicable to arbitration contracts.
- Doctoral research in progress on arbitration, legal legitimacy, and algorithmic transformation.

This research is grounded in a central question: how legal systems preserve legitimacy while adapting to institutional complexity and technological change.
